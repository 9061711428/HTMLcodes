Java Full Stack Program


State:Kerala
Public Partnership:Kudumbasree,DDU GKY

















Appex has collaborated with a kudumbasree and DDU GKY to provide Full stack training for students from Kerala. This 6-month program is a fully residential training program,where the students will be the part of a 4-month classroom training followed by 2 month internship,\ post which students would be provided with on-campus training opportunities.FACE already has tie-ups with various enterprises to priovide up to 300 software developers.They will be sourced from this training pool.The average package for this requirement is 3.5LPA.
